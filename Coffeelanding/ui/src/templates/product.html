Product Features

Cooling Features

Users can experience a range of temperatures for their coffee.
Coffee is brewed hot and goes through precooled multistage cooling.
System is electrically cooled through peltiers, heat sinks, and fans.
Insulated thermoses can keep coffee cold for up to 6 hours.

IoT & Security Features

Connect to the coffee maker through browser and app.
Set coffee temperature and time to be ready by.
Unique UUID generated QR code for each coffee maker.
User log in on browser or app for control.
Secure rest database storing user newsletter and account.
Secure MQTT communication between coffee maker and database.

Website and Subscription Features

Website and landing page for newsletter and deals.
