It is difficult to get iced coffee without either sacrificing convenience or taste.

Going to a coffee shop may result in long minutes waiting in line.
Using ice cubes dilutes the taste due to oxidization.
Putting your coffee in your fridge overnight changes its flavor profile.
Cold brew takes 10+ hours to make properly and isn't iced coffee.

We understand the pain point, Polar Coffee solves it
Our coffee maker brews coffee hot, and delivers coffee freezing cold to the pot.
Hot or cold coffee options are available to those who like both.
IoT capabilities makes your experience conveninent with an app and website.
Affordable and beautiful, we have a compact and efficient design.
